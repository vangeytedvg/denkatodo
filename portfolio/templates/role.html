SCAMPY

The acronym for this application written in Python and PyQt5 means Scan And Mail with Python. I wrote this program for a friend of mine who has little experience with mail, scanners and computers usage in general. The program allows creating an email, scan de the documents through SANE and the use Yagmail to send the email and the scanned documents via Gmail.
